About Me!

I am a woman of 27 years old. I love my life, it's fun and exciting in its own way! I don't think I would change anything about it. I have four beautiful children and a wonderful husband. My life hasn't always been kind to me though.

My story started rough and I definetly would not change my past. It's who I am and how I became me.

In this blog I will go through my life chapter by chapter and let you get to know me as a person. Let you live and breath my heartaches with me. It will almost be like reading a book about my life, except it will be a website instead of a book.
